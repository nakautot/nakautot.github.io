Welcome to Karnstadt

A forgotten town clinging to the edge of civilization, Karnstadt stands as a testament to what remains after the Catastrophe. Its black stone library towers over weathered streets, while Baron Veynar rules from his cliff-top manse. Here, merchants trade in sparse goods, guards patrol with dwindling morale, and whispers of the sealed Vaults beneath the Library draw the curious and the desperate alike.
